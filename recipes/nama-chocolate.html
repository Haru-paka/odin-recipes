Creamy Chocolate

A Japanese style creamy chocolate that melts in your mouth

Surprisingly easy to make! The Measure is [Whipping Cream : Chocolate = 1:2] No need the oven, so it's also good for making with kids!

Ingredients

  • Whipping Cream
  • Chocolate
  • Cocoa Powder

Steps

  1. Cut the chocolate in tiny pieces
  2. Heat the whipping cream in a pan (*not boiling)
  3. While heating the whipping cream, prepare a container and put the food wrap on
  4. Put the tiny chocolate in a heated whipping cream and stir until it melts
  5. Once the chocolate is well melted and well mixed with cream, pour it into the container
  6. Put it in the fridge for a night and make it cool and harden
  7. After it hardens, cut into small pieces
  8. Dust with cocoa powder
  9. Enjoy!
