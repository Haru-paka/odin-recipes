Cheesecake

Just mixing everything and bake it!

This is my mother's recipe that I grew up with. Easy to make and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Whipping Cream 200cc
  • Cream Cheese 200g
  • Sugar 100g
  • Eggs 3
  • Flour 3 table spoon
  • Salt a pinch
  • Lemon Juice 1 table spoon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 230C/446F.
  2. Put every ingredient into a bowl and mix well.
  3. Put it into a round springform pan
  4. Bake it for 30 minutes.
  5. Let it cool.
