Muffin

Cannot go wrong! Basic Muffin that I make every week for my family. Not too sweet, just PERFECT!

Easy to make with simple ingredients. You can vary it with different fruits and jams!

Ingredients

  • Salted Butter 100g
  • Sugar 160g
  • Eggs 2
  • Flour 240g
  • Baking Powder 2 teaspoon
  • Milk 100cc
  • Vanilla Essence 6 drops

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C or 356F.
  2. Put the butter in a bowl and knead it until it will be creamy.
  3. Put sugar into it and mix it well enough to let air in.
  4. In another bowl, beat 2 eggs and mix well.
  5. Put mixed eggs into step3 separating 2 to 3 times and mix well every time.
  6. Put milk and vanilla essence into it and mix them.
  7. Put flour and baking powder into it and mix them. *Don't mix it too much, the batter hardens.
  8. Fill muffin cases two-thirds full and bake it for 20 to 25 minutes.
